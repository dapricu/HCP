How to Rise Your Draft Stock



BE ACTIVE


The number one requirement to be chosen as high as possible in the draft is this one: earn as much TPE as possible.

  • Get your cap weekly.
  • Write your Career Point Tasks.
  • Make your seasonal Ranking.
  • Participate in the weekly games.
  • Copy TacticalHammer's predictions in the offseason.
  • Send your mock draft on time.
  • Sumbmit your EFL and ECFA Fantasy teams.
  • Pay attention to your the LockerRoom #tpe-opportunities channel.
  • Try to land a job.
  • Receive some guidance when making your player's build.

SHOW YOUR ACTIVITY


TPE is important, but so is participating in the community. Imagine two players at the same level, but one can help to improve the LR environment and it's fun to have him around and the other, not so much. Which player do you think will be chosen first?

  • Participate in the team LR.
  • Try to contribute in the EFL Discord Server
  • Read, comment and react to Forum's point tasks.
  • Don't be afraid to discuss league direction.

On top of this, the more you involve yourself in the league, the funnier it is.

BE NICE


It's easy. GMs won't prioritize someone who is known to cause problems.

  • Do not try to bait people.
  • Walk away from dramas.
  • Trash-talk players, not users.
  • Rule of thumb, trash-talk IF you are friends with the user.
  • Keep an open and respectful mind about real life topics.

NAIL THE SCOUTING


If you have suceeded with the above points you have most of the job done, but there's still some points where you can make a difference. GMs will usually send draft interviews to the prospects they might be interested in. How you answer these can mean a lot.

  • Answer GM questions with complete honesty and keep your word.
  • Loyalty is very important. If you are willing to stay loyal under certain conditions, make them known.
  • Make clear what you want and what do you expect from the team.
  • If you don't want to play for a team, let the GM know.
  • Let them know that you are willing to sign for the minimum salary if that's the case.
  • Ask your AD for a quote on you or a recommendation.

STAY UP TO DATE


GMs and League staff will always be there to help, but it's always a plus if the prospect knows how to find his way.

  • Learn to navigate the Forum and find your own answers.
  • Keep track of the EFL, not only the ECFA.
  • Learn about All Time Greats and current bests. Aim high.
